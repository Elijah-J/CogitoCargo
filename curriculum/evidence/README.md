# Evidence Appendices

Full grounding for new EduRatchet-2 Rust move lessons lives here as
`NNN-short-slug.md`.

Each appendix should include:

- cited Rust corpus sources and the exact claims they support;
- local probe commands, toolchain details, and relevant output;
- direct prerequisite claims used by the lesson;
- negative or contrast probes when the lesson makes a contrastive claim,
  or a short reason one was not needed.

The learner-facing lesson should link here instead of replaying the full
audit trail.
